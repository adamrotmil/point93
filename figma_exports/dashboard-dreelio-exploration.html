Borrowed from Dreelio

Sky-to-vellum atmosphere, thick white frame, soft floating pills, and a rounded editorial type voice. Those are the cues that make the reference feel lightweight and memorable.

Adapted for Point93

The playful clouds became softer atmospheric mist, the CTA stayed near-black, and the dashboard cards remain structured so the product still feels credible for private-wealth advisors and brokers.

What To Evaluate

Whether the softer shell makes Point93 feel more expensive and distinct, or whether we should keep the shell and type language but reduce the blue atmosphere in the next pass.
